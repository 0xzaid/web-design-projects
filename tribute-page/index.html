Kendrick Lamar Duckworth

an American rapper and songwriter.
one of the most influential hip hop artists of his generation

  • 1987: Kendrick Lamar is born in Compton, California.
  • 2003: Kendrick Lamar releases his first mixtape, "Youngest Head Nigga in Charge (Hub City Threat: Minor of the Year)".
  • 2010: Kendrick Lamar releases his first independent album, "Overly Dedicated".
  • 2012: Kendrick Lamar's major label debut album, "good kid, m.A.A.d city", is released and becomes a commercial and critical success.
  • 2015: Kendrick Lamar releases his third album, "To Pimp a Butterfly", which wins him five Grammy Awards, including Best Rap Album and Best Rap Song for "i".
  • 2017: Kendrick Lamar releases his fourth album, "DAMN.", which wins him the Pulitzer Prize for Music, making him the first non-classical or jazz artist to win the award.
  • 2018: Kendrick Lamar wins seven Grammy Awards, including Best Rap Album for "DAMN." and Best Rap Song for "HUMBLE.".
  • 2019: Kendrick Lamar and Disney release "Black Panther: The Album, Music from and Inspired By", with Lamar serving as executive producer. The album wins the Grammy Award for Best Score Soundtrack for Visual Media.
  • 2020: Kendrick Lamar releases his fifth album, "untitled unmastered.", which is a collection of previously unreleased demos from the recording sessions for "To Pimp a Butterfly".
  • 2021: Kendrick Lamar collaborates with The Weeknd on the song "Pray for Me" for the "Black Panther" soundtrack, which wins the Grammy Award for Best Song Written for Visual Media.
  • 2022: Kendrick Lamar releases his sixth album, "Mr Morale & The Big Steppers", which becomes a critical and commercial success and wins the Grammy Award for Best Rap Album.
